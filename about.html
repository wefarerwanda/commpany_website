WELFARE INITIATIVE ORGANIZATION (WIO)

National Non-Governmental Organization in Rwanda

Who We Are

We are a national non-governmental organization operating in Rwanda dedicated to improving the wellbeing of the population.

Our Mission

To promote population wellbeing through health, agriculture, and environmental initiatives.

Our Vision

To support people toward self-sufficiency and socio-economic development.

What We Do

We assist vulnerable persons facing serious challenges that affect their wellness, particularly in nutrition, health services, and livelihood support.

Areas of Intervention

  • Agriculture and Livestock
  • Health and Nutrition
  • Environmental Protection

Our Clients

All persons experiencing serious problems that block their socio-economic development and wellbeing.

Objectives

To promote activities related to environmental protection, public health, nutrition, agriculture, and livestock development, and to fight against malnutrition.
